Doe De Date

With this project, I was tasked with conceptualizing and creating a new design every week for three weeks. This was a challenging experience that required me to think quickly and creatively, and to adapt to changing requirements and feedback. Through this process, I developed my ability to iterate on past designs using feedback and rapidly establish new concepts in response to new design challenges. This experience taught me the importance of adaptability and the ability to work efficiently in a dynamic work environment.

I was able to come up with three satisfying designs, one of which was a redesign and an overall improvement of the concept developed in the first week of the design sprint. This project not only helped me to develop my design skills, but also my ability to work under pressure and meet tight deadlines. I believe that this experience has prepared me for the fast-paced and ever-changing nature of the business world and I am confident in my ability to bring value to any organization and make meaningful contributions to its success. Whether working on a solo project or as part of a team, I am dedicated to delivering excellent results and consistently exceeding expectations.

Project Beyond

Project Beyond was one of my most recent large-scale team projects, in which we were required to utilize skills from various disciplines over a period of ten weeks. These skills included working with databases, utilizing Arduino technology to create physical prototypes, and applying various product development techniques.

As a team, we developed a conceptual product called the Volumefriend. The purpose of this product is to provide teachers, schools, and other educational institutions with a non-intrusive and playful solution to make open spaces quieter. The Volumefriend aims to improve the learning environment and make it more conducive to concentration and focus.
